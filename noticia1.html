Parking Ford

On 26/4/2024 a serious argument occurred that resulted in the breakage of the entrance door in Block B of the building.

Specifically, the aggressive worker, in a fit of rage over the argument provoked by the van parked at the community ford, forcefully collapsed the pedestrian entrance gate to the community.

When the neighbor found out about that, he frankly helped the community by successfully fixing the door.
